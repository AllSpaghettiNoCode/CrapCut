How to use:

Simply preview the voice you want (includes Pro voices) and then click the "Download Preview" button.

The last sampled voice will be automatically saved below, so if you refreshed the page, just click the "Download" link to download the last saved voice.

Note: This can only be used on the CapCut site, not the application.

To prevent audio expiration, every 12 hours, each saved audio down below will be deleted. A future update will be added to allow to adjust this.
